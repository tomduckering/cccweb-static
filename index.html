Clapham Central Church

Thanks for visiting out website. Sadly we're not running a Sunday service in Clapham at the moment.

We are however part of the Co-Mission network. It's a family of great Bible teaching, Christ adoring churches in central and south west London. We'd be really happy to recommend some to you:

  • Christ Church Earlsfield - A number of us have gone with our minister to our sister church in Earlsfield. They're a friendly church who meet in the morning with mix of ages and backgrounds.
  • The Boathouse Putney - a few others have gone to the join a small church in Putney. It's a small, relatively young church who meet in the evenings near the river in Putney.
  • Christ Church Mayfair - others have gone here. It's a larger central London church with services in the morning (good for families) and evening (good for students and young professionals).
  • Christ Church Balham - some have joined this church. They are a well established church with people from a variety of backgrounds. Their main meeting is in the morning and they have a supplimentary evening service.

There are still a number of us in the Clapham area. If you'd like to get in touch, we'd be happy to hear from you. You can email us at: info@claphamchurch.org.uk
